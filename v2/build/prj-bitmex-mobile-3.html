Trollbox moderation

Trollbox is a real time chat interface for online traders to exchange ideas and trading strategies. Over the years, the Trollbox had become a cesspool of bad actors and misinformation that was drowning knowledgable traders' voices.

Learning from other exchanges like Poloniex (where trollbox moderation was perceived by users as censorship free speech), I recommended introducing a democratic moderation that leaves Users in control.

Further research and interviews on this subject, led me to propose a simple mute functionality instead of a full blown moderation feature.

Design Challenges
  • Moderation of any kind is perceived as censorship in the crypto world
  • Reasons users find another person offensive is very subjective
  • Necessity of a HIME solution

Wins & Innovations
Most Significant Contribution

Early investigations, SME interview, and analyzing support tickets revealed that a full blown moderation system may be an overkill. On my recommendation, a mute functionality was built and deployed to production and we measured the response metrics.

Most Significant Win
