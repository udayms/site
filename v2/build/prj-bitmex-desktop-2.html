Security Centre

BitMEX

One stop shop for all Security settings

BitMEX is one of the few exchanges that haven't been hacked since going live. This is partly since we take security very seriously and have built a host of different settings that the customer can configure to add layers of security to their account.

As the exhcnage grew, these security features were added on a need basis and located across multiple pages in the user account pages. This project started out as a solution to increase 2FA adoption on the platform. During design research, I discovered that many of our users aren't even aware of these security settings. I proposed a single screen for all security settings and rallied the support of multiple teams including security, product, program, support and compliance teams to get this project on the roadmap and build it out.

Customer Goals
  • Safely trade on the platform without having to be worried about the safety of their funds or trading activities
Product Goals
  • Increase 2FA adoption on the platform from the current 18%
  • Help users understand their options to keep their account and funds safe from account take-overs.
  • Make it easier for users to recover access to their accounts if they lose their 2FA mechanism.
Design Goals
  • Increase discoverability of security features
  • Educate users on what each of these settings mean.
  • Simplify the flow of adding a 2FA device.
Wins & Innovations
Most Significant Contribution

Proposed a 4 phase strategy to increase 2FA on the platform that involved - Consolidation of security features, Passive encouragement, Aggressive encouragement, and Enforcement of security standards

Most Significant Win

Increased 2FA adoption by <bold>33%</bold> and reduced support tickets significantly within 2 weeks
